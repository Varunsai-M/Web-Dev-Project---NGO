About the Job Fair

A generous selection of large, medium and small women friendly companies’ leaders and recruitment teams interested to meet women professionals, will come together at this Mahila Udyoga Mela to hire women. A unique career fair for women to boost their job search and meet senior leaders in companies hiring now. How you will be benefitted?
  • Larger job pool to choose from.
  • Women have proved to be better managers with higher productivity and multitasking skills.
  • Find a job with the convenience of proximity to your residence.
    • What are you waiting for? Register today!

    Our Vision and Mission

    Our vision is to build a nation where women are contributing equally to the National GDP as well as leading the society towards higher Gross National Happiness. Our belief is that when women step forward, the society takes a leap.

    • Our mission is two fold:
    • Empower women with knowledge and techniques to aim high, to develop skills and to achieve their potential leading to personal fulfilment.
    • Build a platform to showcase achievements of 1000+ women who have excelled in their fields; women can interact with, draw inspiration and learn from these living examples of success.
